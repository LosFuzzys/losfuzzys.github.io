Welcome to LosFuzzys' webpage

LosFuzzys ist a CTF team located in Graz, Europe.

We are a group of people interested in information security, which occasionally participates in CTFs.

You are located in Graz? You are interested in competitive information security competitions? You want to participate? Feel free to reach out to us!

  • IRC: #LosFuzzys @ oftc.net
  • Mailinglist: losfuzzys@htu.tugraz.at
